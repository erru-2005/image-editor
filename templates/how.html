How to Use Edit Monkey

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Click on the "Select an Image to Edit" button and choose a file from your device.

Step 2: Choose an Editing Option

From the dropdown menu, select an operation such as Grayscale, Sepia, Blur, Edge Detection, or Format Conversion.

Step 3: Process the Image

Click on the "Submit" button to apply your chosen edits. Your image will be processed instantly.

Step 4: Download Your Edited Image

Once processing is complete, you will receive a link to download the modified image.
